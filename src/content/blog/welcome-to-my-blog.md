Welcome to my blog! I'm **excited** to finally have a space to share my journey as a software engineer—what I’m building, learning, and thinking about along the way.

## What You’ll Find Here

I’ll be writing about things that matter most to me as a developer:

- **Tech deep dives** – React, Next.js, and other tools I use  
- **Project insights** – the wins, the mistakes, and the lessons learned  
- **Career reflections** – strategies, growth, and navigating the industry  
- **Thoughts on tech** – trends, community, and where the field is headed  

## Why I’m Writing

When I started coding, blog posts and tutorials from others helped me make sense of complex topics. This blog is my way of giving back—sharing knowledge, connecting with other developers, and documenting my growth.  

## What’s Next

Coming soon: posts on React patterns, building scalable backends, and the importance of good code reviews.
